Open Play

Looking to join in on the fun? Our Open Play sessions are the perfect time to meet other players, learn new games, and enjoy the TCG Trinity community.

Schedule

  • Magic: The Gathering – Fridays @ 6 PM (Modern) or Saturdays @ 6pm (Commander)
  • Pokémon – Sundays @ 6 PM
  • Lorcana – Sundays @ 4 PM
  • Yu-Gi-Oh! – Tuesdays @ 6 PM
  • Digimon – Mondays @ 5 PM
  • One Piece – Mondays @ 7 PM

How It Works

Everyone is welcome—no sign-up needed! Bring your decks, minis, or favorite tabletop games and join an existing group or start a new one. We keep a few demo games available if you want to try something new.

Questions?

Check out our Events page to see our monthly calendar! If you have questions, please reach out on Facebook or send us a message using the contact form.
